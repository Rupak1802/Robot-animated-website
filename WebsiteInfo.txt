No canvas shaders or WebGL backgrounds detected.
The site uses:
  • Static flat color backgrounds (#f7f7f7 / white)
  • Full-bleed video thumbnails (via Mux CDN) that

  • High-res photography (via Sanity CDN) as section

layered over video/imagery, not GPU shaders.

  appear one-by-one spelling out task names like:
  "Walk to Person", "Put Away Dishes", "Clean Bathroom"

 FEATURES SECTION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Features are grouped into four named pillars:

── UTILITY ─────────────────────────────
Section headline: "Automate Your Chores"
Copy: "Give your NEO a list of chores, schedule a time
       you want them done and come back to a cleaner
       home everyday."

Demonstrated tasks (via video clips):
  Walk to Person / Put Away Dishes / Clean Bathroom
  (plus additional unspecified chore clips)

Feature highlights:
  • Autonomy — "Get the door"
    Uses Redwood AI (1X's Generalist AI model) for
    learning and repeating tasks. Arrives with basic
    autonomy and grows over time.
  • Scheduled Expert Mode — "Laundry"
    For complex unknown tasks, a 1X Expert remotely
    supervises NEO to teach it new abilities.
  • Voice Interface — Speak naturally to access all
    features without using any device.
  • Mobile App — Manage chore schedule, communicate
    remotely, monitor NEO.
  • Self-Charge — NEO manages its own battery;
    plugs itself in when needed.
  • Remote Control — Pilot NEO from anywhere via
    Mobile App or VR device.
  • Emotive Ear Rings — Communicate NEO's state and
    status (battery, attention, etc.)
  • Boom Box — Use NEO as a mobile Bluetooth speaker.

── DESIGN ───────────────────────────────
Section headline: "Soft and Safe"
Copy: "NEO is designed for gentle and safe interactions,
       making it perfect for people and the home."

Feature highlights:
  • Light — Fewer components, lightweight build.
  • Quiet — Operates quieter than a modern refrigerator
             (22 dB audible noise).
  • Soft — Deformable 3D lattice wraps internal
            components for cushioning.
  • Gentle — Tendon-driven actuators create safe movements.
  • Soft Body — All hardware wrapped in custom 3D lattice
                polymer for safety.
  • Safe Movements — 1X Tendon Drive actuation: precise,
                     low-energy movements.
  • Pinch Proof — Joints covered; surface entirely pinch-proof.
  • Body Language — Understands and responds to human
                    gestures and expressions.
  • Comfortable to be Around — Designed to complement
                               your living space.
  • Machine Washable — Soft suit and shoes made from
                       machine-washable nylon.

── COMPANION ────────────────────────────
Section headline: "Laugh and Learn"
Copy: "Ask NEO questions and get instant access to
       knowledge, personalized intelligence, or even a joke."

Feature highlights:
  • Smart — Ask anything (history, recipes, advice) and
             get real-time answers.
  • Contextual Help — Pairs visual/spatial awareness
                      with memory to personalize interactions.
  • Fun — Shares jokes, stories, and games for lively,
           natural conversations.

── INTELLIGENCE ─────────────────────────
Section headline: "Grows With You"
Copy: "NEO is built for full autonomy. Early Access offers
       foundational autonomy, and with continued use, NEO
       learns and unlocks new capabilities."

AI feature highlights:
  • Redwood AI — 1X's proprietary vision language model
                 for learning and performing chores.
  • Built-in LLM — Understands, reasons, and converses.
  • Audio Intelligence — Interprets audio cues and
                         conversational context.
  • Memory — Retains information to personalize experience.
  • Visual Intelligence — Uses visual input to enhance
                          conversations.
  • Fully Mobile — AI-driven navigation to wherever needed.
